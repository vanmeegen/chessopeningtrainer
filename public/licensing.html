Lichess Chess Openings
github.com/lichess-org/chess-openings — CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain)

Opening names, ECO codes, and defining move sequences (~3,600 variations). Dedicated to the public domain.

en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Chess_Opening_Theory — CC BY-SA 3.0

Strategic annotations and move explanations are derived from Wikibooks content contributed by Wikibooks editors and licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 license. Any derived annotation content in this application is therefore also available under CC BY-SA 3.0.

@chess-openings/eco.json
github.com/JeffML/eco.json — MIT License — Jeff Lowery

Encyclopedia of Chess Openings data (15,800+ positions) used to extend short opening lines with continuation moves.
